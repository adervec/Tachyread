## ⚠️ Photosensitivity / seizure warning

This app displays text **flashing rapidly, one word at a time, at speeds up to
~1500 words per minute**, and includes animated faces, neon/pulsing color
themes, and blinking pointer glyphs. **Rapidly flashing or flickering visuals
can trigger seizures in a small number of people, including those with
photosensitive epilepsy who may have no prior history.**

If you or anyone using this app has a history of epilepsy or seizures, consult a
doctor before use. **Stop immediately and seek medical attention** if you
experience dizziness, altered vision, eye or muscle twitching, loss of
awareness, disorientation, or convulsions. Use lower speeds, calmer themes, and
take frequent breaks.

## Eye strain & comfort

Rapid reading and prolonged screen use can cause eye strain, fatigue, or
headaches. Take regular breaks and stop if you feel discomfort.

## No efficacy claims (speed reading)

This app implements **RSVP** (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) with an
**ORP** (Optimal Recognition Point).
The benefits of speed-reading techniques, and the trade-off between reading
speed and comprehension, are **debated in the scientific literature**.

The WPM, "reading efficiency," "coverage," accuracy, and similar numbers shown
in this app are **heuristic measurements for personal interest and
gamification** — not a scientific, clinical, educational, or cognitive
assessment. They are **not a guarantee** of comprehension, retention, learning,
or any improvement in reading or typing skill. The typing/speaking minigames and
stat readouts are for fun, not professional instruction.
